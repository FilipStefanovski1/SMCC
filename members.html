Members

A community of Swedish and Macedonian companies

SMCC unites companies of all sizes – from start-ups and SMEs to large corporations and institutions – that share an interest in the Swedish and Macedonian markets.

Members benefit from a structured platform for networking, business development and dialogue with public authorities. The Chamber provides a safe and neutral space where new ideas and collaborations can emerge.

Our members represent a broad range of sectors including manufacturing, ICT, healthcare, green technologies, professional services, education and creative industries. This diversity creates unique opportunities for cross-sector partnerships.

Why companies join SMCC

  • Access to curated networking events and delegations.
  • Practical support when entering a new market or expanding operations.
  • Visibility through Chamber communication channels and joint activities.
  • Possibility to influence topics of common interest through working groups and committees.

Membership categories

SMCC offers several membership categories tailored to the size and profile of your organization, ranging from smaller companies to large corporate partners. Bronze, Silver and Gold tiers provide increasing levels of visibility and involvement.

Detailed information about the practical benefits, invoicing and terms can be provided upon request. The Chamber also offers strategic partnership packages for organizations that wish to be closely involved in the development of activities.
