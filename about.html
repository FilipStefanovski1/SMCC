About SMCC

Building bridges between Sweden and North Macedonia

The Swedish–Macedonian Chamber of Commerce (SMCC) is a non-profit, membership-based organisation working to promote trade, investment and long-term cooperation between the two countries.

SMCC serves as a neutral platform for companies, institutions and entrepreneurs who see opportunities in both markets. Through delegations, networking, advisory support and communication activities, we connect the right stakeholders and help turn ideas into concrete cooperation.

Mission

What we do

SMCC promotes trade, investment and cooperation between Sweden and North Macedonia by providing a trusted platform for dialogue, joint initiatives and business development.

  • Connecting companies, institutions and stakeholders.
  • Organising delegations, events and business meetings.
  • Providing market insights and practical guidance.
  • Highlighting success stories from both countries.
Vision

Long-term partnerships

Our vision is to contribute to a modern, sustainable and innovation-driven partnership between Sweden and North Macedonia, where companies, institutions and citizens benefit from easier cooperation.

The Chamber’s work is guided by openness, professionalism and long-term relationships. We aim to be a trusted partner for both Swedish and Macedonian stakeholders.

Leadership & Governance

Executive director, board & office

The Chamber is governed by a board consisting of representatives from both Sweden and North Macedonia, supported by an executive director and a dedicated office team.
