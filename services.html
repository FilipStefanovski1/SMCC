Services

Support for your cross-border journey

SMCC offers a mix of standard and tailor-made services to help companies navigate and succeed in the Swedish and Macedonian markets.

Market entry guidance

Initial guidance on regulations, business culture, labour market and incentive schemes, along with signposting to specialised service providers.

Partner & client search

Identification of potential partners, clients or suppliers based on your profile, followed by introductions and support for first meetings.

Delegations & study visits

Organisation of targeted delegations and study visits, including programmes, logistics support and follow-up towards concrete outcomes.

Event co-creation

Joint organisation of seminars, workshops and conferences on topics such as green transition, digitalisation and innovation.

Communication & visibility

Promotion of your activities and success stories through the Chamber’s channels and events, in cooperation with partners.

Project collaboration

Support in forming consortia and connecting with relevant stakeholders for EU-funded or bilateral projects.
